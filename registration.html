To Register For an Individual Adult/Junior:

- To register for an individual adult or junior member, choose the club membership from the drop down menu below. 
- You'll need insurance to join the club. To choose the insurance that fits your goals use the "Choose Member Services" drop down menu. If you do not wish to compete, simply select "Non-Competitive"
 - Need to store your boat at the club? Select yes or no in this step.
 - If you would like to join a program, choose the type of program from the program drop down menu, and then select an appropriate practice package. If you don't want to join a program, just select "Will not join a program".
- Have a promo code? Enter it in the box labelled "Promo Code".  Please note that promo codes need to be typed as shown and are validated at checkout.
