To Register For a Family:

Family registration is easy. Follow the steps below to register each family member:

- Register the first family member (usually an adult).

-Choose "Club Membership - Family Rate" from the Club membership drop down menu.

- You'll need insurance to join the club. To choose the insurance that fits your goals use the "Choose CKBC member services fee" drop down menu. If you do not wish to compete, simply select "Non-Competitive"

- Need to store your boat at the club? Select yes or no in this step.

- If you would like to join a program, choose the type of program from the program drop down menu, and then select an appropriate practice package. If you don't want to join a program, just select "Will not join a program".

- Register the second family member (usually the second adult).

- Choose the "Already Paid 2015 Family Club membership" from the dropdown menu.

    -Follow the same steps as you did when registering the first family member.
-For Club Membership choose

   -Please see the Promo Codes list

If you are registering Youth in the family:

- Register the first youth in the family.

    -Follow the same steps as you did when registering the first family member.

     -Please see the Promo Codes list

- Register the second youth in the family.
    -Follow the same steps as you did when registering the first family member.

    -Please see the Promo Codes list

-If you have more than two youth in the family.

    -Continue to use the same procedure as above and choose whether there are 3 or 4 youths in the family.

Family Discount Promo Codes:

    -If two or more family members are joining programs apply: MULTIPRGREG2

    -If three or more family members are joining programs apply: MULTIPRGREG3

    -If four or more family members are joining programs apply: MULTIPRGREG4

        *Please note that the discount only applies when two or more family members are a part of a program (these can be the same program or different programs).
